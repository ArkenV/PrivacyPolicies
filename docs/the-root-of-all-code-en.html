Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

The Root of All Code is an educational app that introduces programmers to non-mainstream programming languages.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

Internet Usage

The app connects to the internet solely to check for app updates via the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

No personal information is sent during these connections.

Local Storage

The app stores your preferred display language on your device so it persists between sessions. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the developer.
